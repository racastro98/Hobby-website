Weight Training

Weight training is a type of strength training that uses weights for resistance. This could be weight machines or free weights, like dumbbells or barbells, at home or a gym. It stresses and strengthens your muscles over time.

What are the benfits of weight training?

Weight training may enhance your quality of life and improve your ability to do everyday activities. Strength training can also protect your joints from injury. Building muscle also can contribute to better balance and may reduce your risk of falls. This can help you maintain independence as you age.

Is weight training a sport?

Weight Training is not a competitive sport, instead is is a training system.However, some people who enjoy this go on to become powerlifters. Powerlifting is a strength sport that consists of three attempts at maximal weight on three lifts: squat, bench press, and deadlift. As in the sport of Olympic weightlifting, it involves the athlete attempting a maximal weight single-lift effort of a barbell loaded with weight plates.

Why do I enjoy weight training?

I enjoy strength training I enjoy strength training for a plethora of reasons. The first reason is the fact that weight lifting is physically empowering, it allows me to build the physique that I want , while I improve my overall fitness levels. The feeling of achieving personal goals boosts my confidence. It is a very effective stress reliever, whenever I have had a long day or I am feeling anxious going to the gym allows me to feel better, this could be due to the endorphins released during a workout, improving my mental health. Despite what social media may show us being a part of the gym means being a part of a community; people are always encouraging me to go harder and if I fail I feel encouraged to keep going and lastly, strength training is a fun way to be competitive within yourself, it is a way to block out the world with your music and focus on something else.
